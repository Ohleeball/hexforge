# High Concept

## Elevator Pitch

A fantasy survival game where you defend a growing hex-grid settlement against endless enemy hordes — alone, no towers — while pushing out across the map to capture territory, earn upgrades, and build a base that thinks like an auto-battler.

## Genre

Vampire Survivors × Dome Keeper × Teamfight Tactics

## Core Fantasy

You are the only thing standing between your settlement and destruction. Every second you spend away building your empire is a second your walls are undefended. Every second you spend defending is a second you're not growing. The tension between those two things is the game.

## What Makes It Different

- **No tower defense.** The player is the only active defender. Buildings produce upgrades and gold, not firepower.
- **Pause-to-build.** Entering your base pauses the game and opens planning mode. Building decisions get the same deliberate attention as auto-battler decisions.
- **Spatial upgrade tree.** Upgrades are buildings placed on a hex grid. Three of the same building merge into a higher tier. Adjacency between buildings creates synergies. Where you build matters as much as what you build.
- **Permanent consequences.** Walls don't repair. Sold buildings lose their blueprint. Every decision leaves a mark on the run.

## Setting

Fantasy. Hex grid map with varied terrain zones. Medieval/arcane aesthetic. Compatible with standard fantasy asset packs.

## Platform Target

PC (prototype). Keyboard and mouse.
